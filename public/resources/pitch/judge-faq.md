# Judge FAQ Prep Sheet

## 1. Technical Questions
- **"How does it scale?"**
  - *Answer:* Discuss your database indexing, serverless functions, or architectural bottlenecks.
- **"Why this tech stack?"**
  - *Answer:* Explain the trade-offs (e.g., speed of development vs. performance).

## 2. Business Questions
- **"What is your monetization strategy?"**
  - *Answer:* Freemium, B2B SaaS, or Transaction-based.
- **"Who is your first customer?"**
  - *Answer:* Reference your SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market).

## 3. Product Questions
- **"What's the hardest thing you built?"**
  - *Answer:* Highlight a specific technical hurdle you overcame during the 48 hours.
- **"How is this different from [Competitor]?"**
  - *Answer:* Pivot back to your Unique Value Proposition.

---
*Tip: Keep answers short (30-45 seconds) and let every team member answer at least one question.*
