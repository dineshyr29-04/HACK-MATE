# Winning Pitch Script (2-Minute Template)

## 0:00 - 0:15 | The Hook & Problem
"Imagine [Persona Name] is trying to [Task]. But right now, they're stuck because [Pain Point]. This is a [Dollar Amount] problem affecting [Number] people worldwide."

## 0:15 - 0:45 | The Solution
"That's why we built **[Project Name]**. It's the first [Category] that helps [Persona] do [Action] without [Friction]. Using [Key Tech], we've made [Process] 10x faster."

## 0:45 - 1:30 | The Demo (Visuals)
"As you can see on the screen, [Persona] simply [Step 1]. Our engine then [Step 2], and within seconds, they have [Result]. No more [Pain Point]."

## 1:30 - 1:45 | The Tech & Scalability
"Under the hood, we're using **[Tech Stack]**. We've designed it to be horizontally scalable and ready for production on Day 1."

## 1:45 - 2:00 | The Team & Close
"Our team consists of [Role] and [Role], and we're passionate about [Industry]. We are [Project Name], and we're making [Industry] better. Any questions?"

---
*Pro Tip: Practice your script 5 times with a timer. Never read from a screen during the live pitch.*
