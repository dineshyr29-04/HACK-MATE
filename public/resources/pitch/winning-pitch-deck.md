# Winning Pitch Deck Structure (10-Slide Template)

## Slide 1: The Hook
- **Visual:** High-quality logo and punchy tagline.
- **Goal:** Grab attention in the first 5 seconds.

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Visual:** A photo or graphic showing the friction.
- **Goal:** Make the audience feel the pain.

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **Visual:** A clean screenshot of your product's core feature.
- **Goal:** Show, don't just tell.

## Slide 4: Demo (The WoW Moment)
- **Action:** A 1-minute live demo or video walkthrough.
- **Goal:** Prove that it actually works.

## Slide 5: Market Opportunity
- **Data:** TAM, SAM, SOM numbers.
- **Goal:** Show that this can be a real business.

## Slide 6: Competitive Landscape
- **Visual:** 2x2 matrix or a simple comparison table.
- **Goal:** Explain why you win.

## Slide 7: Technical Architecture
- **Visual:** Simple flow diagram of your tech stack.
- **Goal:** Show professional engineering.

## Slide 8: Business Model
- **Plan:** How do you make money?
- **Goal:** Sustainability.

## Slide 9: The Team
- **Team:** High-res photos and role titles.
- **Goal:** Build trust.

## Slide 10: The Ask & Thank You
- **Action:** What do you need? (e.g., Investment, Users, Feedback)
- **Goal:** A professional close.

---
*Tip: One idea per slide. Keep text minimal (under 20 words per slide).*
