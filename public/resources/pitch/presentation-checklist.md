# Final Presentation Checklist

## 1. Physical / Environmental Check
- [ ] Good lighting (faces are visible).
- [ ] Background is clean or blurred.
- [ ] **Mics are working** (most important).
- [ ] Screen resolution is readable (no small text).

## 2. Content Proofing
- [ ] Slide sequence flows logically.
- [ ] Demo video loads instantly (no buffer).
- [ ] Final 'Thank You' slide has contact info or GitHub link.

## 3. The Pitch Prep
- [ ] Script is rehearsed (under 3 mins).
- [ ] One 'WoW' moment is highlighted.
- [ ] A 'Next Steps' plan is included for future growth.

---
*Tip: Smile, look at the camera (not the screen), and have fun. Your energy is contagious.*
