# Market Size Guide (TAM, SAM, SOM)

## 1. TAM (Total Addressable Market)
*The total market demand for a product or service.*
- Example: "Global AI Software Market"
- Metric: $150 Billion

## 2. SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
*The segment of the TAM within your geographical reach.*
- Example: "North American AI Software Market for SMEs"
- Metric: $20 Billion

## 3. SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
*The portion of SAM that you can capture.*
- Example: "Short-term target of 5% of US SME AI market"
- Metric: $1 Billion

---
*Tip: Be realistic. Investors and judges prefer credible, bottom-up calculations over inflated top-down numbers.*
