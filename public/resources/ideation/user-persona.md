# User Persona Template

## 1. Identity
- **Name:** (e.g., "Developer Dave")
- **Role:** (e.g., Junior Frontend Developer)
- **Age/Location:** (e.g., 22, San Francisco)

## 2. Goals & Motivations
- What are they trying to achieve?
- What drives their daily decisions?

## 3. Frustrations & Pain Points
- What annoys them about current solutions?
- What are the "deal-breakers"?

## 4. Tech Savviness
- Are they an expert or a novice?
- What tools do they already use?

---
*Tip: Give your persona a real face and name. It helps the team stay focused on the user.*
