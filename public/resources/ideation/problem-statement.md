# Problem Statement Template (1-Page)

## 1. The Ideal State
*Describe what the world or the process looks like when the problem doesn't exist.*
- What is the goal?
- Who is the primary beneficiary?
- What are the key metrics of success in this ideal world?

## 2. The Reality
*Describe the current situation, the pain points, and the friction.*
- What is happening right now?
- What are the specific hurdles?
- Who is suffering the most from this? (Target Audience)

## 3. The Consequences
*Explain the impact of not solving this problem.*
- Lost time/money/efficiency.
- Emotional or social impact.
- Why is it URGENT to solve this right now?

## 4. The Proposed Solution (High Level)
*Don't go into technical details yet, focus on the "What".*
- How does your solution bridge the gap between Reality and the Ideal State?
- What is the Core Unique Value Proposition?

---
*Tip: A great problem statement makes a judge node their head in agreement before you even show the product.*
