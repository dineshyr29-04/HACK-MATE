## SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- What makes us unique?
- What are our unfair advantages (speed, cost, niche)?

### Weaknesses
- Where are we vulnerable during the hackathon?
- What features are we skipping for the MVP?

### Opportunities
- What market gap are we filling?
- Is there a specific trend we are riding?

### Threats
- Who could easily replicate this?
- What are the external risks?

---
*Note: Focus on the "Gap" - where everyone else is failing, and you are winning.*
