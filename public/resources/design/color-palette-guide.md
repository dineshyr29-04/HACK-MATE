# Hackathon Color Palette & Branding Guide

## 1. Modern Tech Palettes

### The "SaaS Premium"
- **Primary:** `#0F172A` (Slate 900)
- **Accent:** `#3B82F6` (Blue 500)
- **Success:** `#10B981` (Emerald 500)
- **Background:** `#F8FAFC` (Slate 50)

### The "Cyberpunk Dark"
- **Background:** `#050505` (True Black)
- **Primary:** `#D9F99D` (Lime 200)
- **Secondary:** `#A855F7` (Purple 500)
- **Text:** `#EDEDED` (Off White)

## 2. Accessibility Checklist
- **Contrast:** Ensure text has at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio against the background.
- **Color Blindness:** Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning (e.g., use icons + colors for errors).
- **Focus States:** Always include visible `:focus` styles for keyboard navigation.

## 3. Font Pairings
- **Headings:** *Inter* or *Outfit* (Black/Bold)
- **Body:** *Inter* or *Roboto* (Regular/Medium)
- **Mono:** *JetBrains Mono* or *Fira Code* (For code snippets)

---
*Tip: Stick to 2 main colors and 1 accent color to keep the design clean and professional.*
