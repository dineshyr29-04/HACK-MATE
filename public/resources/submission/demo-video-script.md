# Hackathon Demo Video Script

## 0:00 - 0:15 | The Intro (The Hook)
- **Speaker:** "Hi, we're [Team Name], and we're solving [Problem] with [Project Name]."
- **Visual:** Show the high-level hero section and branding.

## 0:15 - 0:45 | The Problem & Friction
- **Speaker:** "Every day, [Persona] struggles with [Pain Point]. Current solutions are [Too slow / Too expensive / Too hard]."
- **Visual:** Show a quick clip of the 'Old Way' vs. your landing page.

## 0:45 - 2:00 | The Master Demo (The Hero Content)
- **Action:** Record your screen while performing the core action.
- **Narrathon:** "Simply click here, upload your [File], and our AI engine instantly [Action]. See how fast that was?"
- **Visual:** Screen recording with smooth cursor movements.

## 2:00 - 2:45 | Tech & Innovation
- **Speaker:** "We built this using [Stack]. What makes us unique is how we [Specific Tech Innovation]."
- **Visual:** 3-second flash of the code or architecture diagram.

## 2:45 - 3:00 | Conclusion & Impact
- **Speaker:** "With [Project Name], we're making [Industry] 10x more efficient. Check us out on GitHub!"
- **Visual:** Team photo and "Thank You" slide.

---
*Tip: Record audio separately with a good mic. Use zoom-ins on the video to highlight small UI elements.*
